If the immediate cause of death arose from or as a complication of an error or accident in surgery or other medical procedure or treatment, it is important to report:

  1. the result of the complication or error,
  2. the complication or error, and
  3. the medical procedure was performed, and
  4. what condition was being treated.

In some jurisdictions, it may be required to report these deaths to the medical examinier or coroner.
