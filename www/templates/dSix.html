Diabetes mellitus may be reported as a cause of death, either as one of the direct causes of death in Part I or as a contributing cause of death in Part II. When indicating diabetes as a cause of death, specify whether Type I (and whether immune-mediated or idiopathic), Type II, gestational diabetes, monogenic diabetes (and whether neonatal diabetes or maturity-onset diabetes of the young [MODY]), or some other specific type of diabetes, if possible.
